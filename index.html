Who We Are

Nigeria's Most Trusted Roofing Company

What We Offer

Our Roofing Services

From supply to installation, we deliver roofing solutions built for Nigerian weather and built to last.

Stone-Coated Roofing Tiles

Premium stone-coated roofing tiles (Gerald Roofing) designed for beauty and long-term durability. These roofs offer a modern finish while withstanding harsh weather conditions.

  • 50-year color guarantee
  • Elegant and stylish appearance
  • Highly durable and weather-resistant

Long-Span Aluminum Roofing Sheets

Strong and cost-effective aluminum roofing sheets ideal for both residential and commercial buildings. Built to handle large structures with ease.

  • Lightweight and rust-resistant
  • Affordable and long-lasting
  • Suitable for large structures

Sales & Installation of Water Collectors

We supply and install high-quality rainwater collection systems designed to integrate seamlessly with your roofing structure — capturing and managing water efficiently for any property.

  • Supply of premium water collector units
  • Professional installation service
  • Seamless integration with all roof types
